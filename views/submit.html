Guidelines
  • Please upload the following 3 attachments before we come back to you. Synopsis and strength of the book, Two sample chapters and Curriculum Vitae of the Author
  • Submission should be in word format only
  • Bookbanyan accepts submissions in English in the fiction genre only at the moment
  • Please allow at least 2 weeks for your manuscript to be considered for publication.
  • We request you not to send us hard copies unless specifically requested by our Editors.
  • Bookbanyan will not be responsible for any loss or damage to unsolicited material. Manuscripts will not be returned by the publishing house irrespective of the outcome of the review process.
