Summary

The story of a young soul who dared to dream, and whose patriotic passions were ignited only to be left down by the societal dynamics. A soul which places the love of motherland over its own body. The END of a saga and hopefully a beginning too.... An experiment which every Indian hopes will succeed...

Few lines from the Book

I leave college as an Indian and then I become a Hindu, a Muslim, a Brahmin, a Sudra, a Bengali or a Madrasi. I become everything else conceivable in the narrowest mind but not Indian.“ This probably is applicable to every student passing out from college.

Just as a she goat maybe milked at any time at one’s own will, so can these women be enjoyed at any time at one’s discretion.

Brave and patriotic

Suicide at IIT is an audacious and brave write-up. It will compel every reader to inculcate feelings of patriotism and empathy in him, lined with laces of helplessness. The novella begins with the suicide of a bright IIT student, Siddharth. Investigation begins but proofs and evidences perplex Justice Sahu. Siddharth was a genius in his academics and inter curricular activities, his personal life had no problems and he had lots of admirers and friends.

Siddharth had a curious, reflective and an overly inquisitive mind. The reason behind the suicide is sure to startle every reader and fill him with pathos and patriotism. This book is a must read for every Indian heart who has an irrevocable love for his/her motherland.

The best part of the book is that it is crisp, compact and can be finished within a maximum of three hours. The content of the novella is fresh, intriguing and touching. A good book is that which leaves you with a haunted feel after you complete it. Suicide at IIT is one such book. I am still bewitched by the book’s spell. I want to congratulate the author for writing a book which can bring about a change in the society hopefully. I recommend this book to everyone. This is no ordinary book; it mirrors the reality and grievances of each one of us. I want the author to write many such books for the betterment of this country.
